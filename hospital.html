About Us

For over 25 years, Aaradhya hospital has been a pillar of compassionate and quality healthcare in the community. Since our founding in 2000, we have grown into a trusted institution, known for our commitment to medical excellence, patient-centered service, and innovation. From humble beginnings to a full-service hospital offering advanced diagnostics, surgery, maternity, emergency, and specialty care, our journey has been driven by a single mission: to heal, to serve, and to make a lasting difference.
